How to Pass Your Audit
01
Understand every line of your code.No copy-paste without understanding. You will be asked to explain your logic live, line by line if needed.
02
Test your project before the session.Run it fresh, check edge cases, make sure it doesn't crash on obvious inputs. A crash is a fail.
03
Re-read the project requirements the day before.Many students miss simple requirements. The spec is the contract — check every line.
04
Know your time and space complexity.For every function you wrote — O(?) time, O(?) space. If you can't answer this, the audit will be hard.
05
Come with questions.Audits are learning opportunities too. Q&A slots exist for a reason — use them, not just when you're failing.
06
Be on time.More than 5 minutes late and the slot may be released to another student.
