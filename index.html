🚀 Tips to Pass Your Audit
1

Understand every line of your code

No copy-paste without understanding. You'll be asked to explain your logic live.

2

Test your project before the session

Run it fresh, check edge cases, make sure it doesn't crash on obvious inputs.

3

Re-read the project requirements

Many students miss simple requirements. Check the spec again the day before.

4

Come with questions

Audits are learning opportunities too. Q&A slots exist for a reason — use them.

5

Be on time

More than 5 minutes late and the slot may be released to another student.
